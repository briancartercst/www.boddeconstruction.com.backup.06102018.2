SERVICES

Ultimately the measure of a great contractor is in there ability to make the owners dreams of what their home or business should be, come true.  Our experienced team can easily make your ideas into a reality from start to finish.  We believe this process begins with clear communication between the owners, contractor and everyone working on your project.  After all, most disputes in the construction business comes down to one factor, bad communication.  We find the best way to avoid this problem is to take the "hands on approach" described on our home page. 

Drawing & Design: We offer a drawing and design service that uses a combination of a 3D home designs program and old school pencil and paper.  We can easily produce the drawings necessary to obtain building permits and approval from neighborhood architectural review boards for your projects.  

Estimates:  We will gladly provide a written estimate for your project that is valid for thirty days from the date on your estimate.  We also offer a time and materials contract that is used when work must begin immediately or is not possible to estimate.  For example, emergency repairs from storm damage or kitchen design to name a few. 

Honey Do Lists:  Odd jobs around the house, we all have them and we all hate to spend our free time doing them.  A finished honey do list can go a long way in the domestic bliss department and besides if I am working on your list I can put mine off a little longer. 

 Bathroom:  Bathroom work is where our multi skilled employees excel.  After all how many subcontractors can you fit into the average sized bathroom.  We combine luxury finishes, basins, cabinetry and frameless shower doors for the contemporary look or vintage materials and fixtures to complement your historic home. 

Kitchen: Updating your kitchen doesn't necessarily mean a complete remodel if it has a good layout.  Ceramic tile floor and backsplash, cabinet refinishing, painting and countertops are all great options to make the old kitchen appear new.  Let us show you how wall finishes and latest in functional design techniques can spice up your kitchen/dining experience.

Interior Remodeling:  Whether converting unfinished attic into a study/game room, or expanding your home, we'll estimate and construct the upgrade you've been look for.  Our team has extensive expertise in interior remodeling.  Crown molding, paneled and bead board wainscot, entertainment centers, bookcases all milled from solid wood for lasting value. 

Outdoor Living:  We install entry doors, decks, patios, screened-in porches for year-round living.  We can add sitting area so that your home into beautiful functionality.

We over see our projects with a critical eye for detail.  From estimates, construction supervision to punch list and clean-up, we provide scrupulous project management.
